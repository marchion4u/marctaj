Who we are ?

We are merchants who opened their company in 2018, our ambition is to specialize in online retailing
in the European marketplaces in order to access a customer base estimated at several million prospects.

More than a year after our launch, we have reached our customers with our various products
and have as a new objective to increase our supply capacity.

In this context, we are now looking for reliable wholesalers who can supply us to meet the demand of our customers.
